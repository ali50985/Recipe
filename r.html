Pizza

Ingredients

Flour,Cheese,Vegetable


Process

Knead the dough until soft and elastic, then let it rest for a while so it rises nicely.

Roll it out into a circle and place it on a baking tray. Spread the tomato sauce evenly, sprinkle cheese generously, and top with your favorite vegetables. Bake it in a preheated oven until the crust turns golden and the cheese melts to perfection. Serve hot and enjoy!

Pan Cake

Ingredients

Flour,Sugar,Milk


Process

Whisk everything into a smooth batter, making sure there are no lumps. Heat a pan and lightly grease it with butter. Pour a ladle of batter in the center and let it spread out. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip carefully to cook the other side until golden brown. Stack the pancakes, drizzle with syrup or honey, and serve warm with fruits for extra flavor
